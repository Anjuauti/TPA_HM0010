About Us

Campus Exchange is a dedicated platform designed exclusively for students to buy and sell educational resources. We understand the financial challenges that students face and aim to create an affordable, secure, and user-friendly marketplace where students can trade textbooks, stationery, and other learning materials. Our platform ensures that only relevant educational products are listed, keeping the focus on academic needs and providing a trusted space for students to connect and help each other succeed.
